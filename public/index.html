Vergomont Hotel

Estd. early 1900. Rooms available.


The Vergomont reopens its gates in 2018.

Poised and charismatic, the colonial structure gives this holiday home a classic allure. With the charisma of the bygone era intact, The Vergomont, offers three suites with attached bathrooms to accommodate about 10-12 guests. The rooms come with modest boutique décor and all basic amenities to ensure that you have a restful stay at this holiday home.

Located at an altitude of 1219 meters above sea level,surrounded by magnificent mountains, lush green trees with chirping birds and with moderate climate prevailing throughout the year, Vergomont is the ideal holiday destination to relax and rejuvenate. It is in close proximity to Nainital and the drive time from Delhi does not exceed six hours. There are daily trains from Delhi to Kathgodam and Jeolikot is a short drive from there.

One can indulge in various activities like trekking, bird watching, lake visits to Nainital, Bhimtal , visit nearby temples , day trips to higher locations like mukteshwar or simply do nothing but relax in the soulful, quiet ambience the place offers.
