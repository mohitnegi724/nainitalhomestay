Vergomont Holiday Home

Estd. early 1900s. Rooms available .

Back in history around early 1900s, Vergomont Hotel existed as a stop-over for weary travelers commuting to Nainital, higher hills and places of interest in Kumaon by foot or horse-backs.

Present day, Vergomont hotel has been converted into a vacation home offering travelers a unique and intimate holiday stay experience. Guests get a glimpse into an era gone by and a feel of the culture and lifestyle of that time.


Welcome to Vergomont.

Poised and charismatic, the stone walls and wooden roofs give this colonial structure a classic allure. With charisma of the bygone era intact, one experiences nostalgia and reminisces about traditions and lifestyles of those times. Even the trees are over a century old and tell their own stories.

Located at an altitude of 1219 meters above sea level, surrounded by magnificent mountains, chirping birds, lush green trees and temperate climate prevailing throughout the year, Vergomont is an ideal holiday destination to relax and rejuvenate. The distance from Delhi is about 280kms and is a six hour drive. There are several trains from Delhi to Kathgodam including an early morning Shatabdi train. We help arrange taxis for guests if they need one at Kathgodam train station.

We look forward to seeing you at Vergomont this summer.

Property Details

Vergomont offers three suites with attached bathrooms to accommodate about 10-12 guests. The rooms come with boutique décor and all modern amenities to ensure that you have a restful stay at this holiday home.
You can book the entire bungalow for friends and family for a great outing together or you can book individual rooms or suites for smaller groups or individuals.

Room Options:


Delux Family Suite 1

Two large interconnected rooms. Can sleep 4-6 guests. Has an active antique fireplace for winter months. Access to living room, kitchen and front/back gardens

Delux Family Suite 2

One large room connected to a smaller room. Can sleep 3-4 guests. Has an active fireplace for winter months. Access to living room, kitchen and front/back gardens.

Writers Room

A cosy room with an attached study. A lovely antique writing table with a rustic table lamp that entices one to pick up the pen and write something. Access to living room, kitchen and front/back gardens.

THINGS TO DO

We have identified many interesting activities that one can include in their itineraries besides the regular trips to Nainital, Bhimtal lakes, local temples and other on-radar activities. Depending on how much time you have and based upon your appetite for travel and physical excursion, we can arrange the following with prior notice.


Short duration treks and jungle walks. Day trips lasting 3-4 hours in and around Nainital area. Ideal for all age groups as a fun outing.

Adventure sports and fun activities like paragliding, rock climbing, sailing, camping in and around Nainital.

Bird watching workshops with professional instructors. These can be short or long term and for all levels. We will arrange the most ideal location studios.

Longer excursions to locations like Almora, Ranikhet, Kausani, Jageshwar, Mukteshwar, Munsiyari, Pindari Glacier etc. Vergomont serves as a base camp.

Offsite corporate meeting and event venue for small groups. We set up programs that are fun to participate in.

Relax at Vergomont and give yourself some personal time. Pick a book or listen to chirping of birds, rustling of trees and enjoy the lovely breeze that blows.
